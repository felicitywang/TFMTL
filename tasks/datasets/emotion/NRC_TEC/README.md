**Hashtag Emotion Corpus / Twitter Emotion Corpus(TEC)**

**Summary**
Tweets with emotion labels self-labeled with their hashtag annotations.

**Basic stats:**
+ \# items = 21,051
+ \# labels = 6
    - anger
    - disgust
    - fear
    - joy
    - sadness
    - surprise
